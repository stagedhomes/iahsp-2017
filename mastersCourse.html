Each of the Below Days are Very Intense, Highly Educational and Hands On With Barb. There will be Homework Each Evening so that Every Day is the Most Productive Day Possible for You.
Day 1
Your first day with Barb you will prepare a Home Staging bid for a vacant property. After being at the actual house, you will begin to prepare your proposal and begin to put your bid together for the property. Putting your proposal together is a very educational and powerful part of the Masters Course. For, in the next morning you will present your proposal to the class as to how you would Stage the property and present the bid to the client. How you present your proposal is very important for the future business. Barb will critique your presentation and communication in a positive constructive way to help you do the best proposals possible. This hands-on learning experience with Barb will truly give you more ideas of how to prepare and present your bids which will lead to more business for you. On Day One you will learn:
 
  • Home Staging ASPM® communication skills
  • ASPM® Home Staging bids/proposals
  • Preparing an ASPM® Home Staging Proposal for a vacant property
  • Turning your ideas into an ASPM® Home Staging bid for an actual home
  • More about the differences between preparing proposals and consultations, as well as pricing your projects
Day 2
This Day will begin with your Presentation of your Home Staging bid to Barb and the Class.
You will be receiving Positive Feedback/Critique about your Proposal and Presentation from Barb. This is such a valuable part of the ASPM® Course in the personal feedback you receive from Barb and always in a positive way. In the ASPM® Course you learn what to do but then you go into the field and do not have the opportunity for feedback on your work, here in the Masters Course it is one of the most valuable benefits of becoming a Master ASPM®. Then in the afternoon On Day Two you will visit another home with Barb. This home is lived in and occupied. Here you will prepare a Home Staging Consultation! In the house you will have time to write down your thoughts and ideas. When you return to the classroom, you will begin to prepare your consultation...
 
  • ASPM® Home Staging Consultations
  • Preparing an ASPM® Home Staging Consultation for a lived in Property
  • Turning your ideas into a Home Staging Consultation for an actual home
Day 3
Presentation of your Home Staging Consultation to Barb and the Class Receiving Positive Feedback/Critique about your Consultation and Presentation from Barb.
In the afternoon Barb, who is one of the most highly rated gifted speakers alive today, will be sharing her speaking tips, ideas, techniques and abilities with you about giving presentations. As you market yourself and your business, it's important that you give presentations about Home Staging and the services and benefits that ASPM® Staging provides. This is a wonderful opportunity to grow and learn positive speaking techniques and ideas that work! From Barb's 34 years as a professional, award winning speaker, you will come away with ideas galore of how to make and give presentations in RE Offices to build your ASPM® Home Staging business!
  • Who, where, and how to market your business
  • Successful marketing ideas, techniques, and skills
  • Presentation skills
  • Speaking skills, ideas and techniques taught to you by a master speaker, Barb Schwarz
  • 5 Minute Short Talks Presented by Each of You to our ASPM Class
